Basic Payment Scheme (BPS)

BPS application window is open

  • The application window closes on 15 May.
  • You can submit applications after 15 May but late claim penalties apply.
  • You cant submit an application after 9 June using this system. To apply after this daye, you'll need to contact the Rural Payments Agency.

Apply for BPS

As part of the new Common Agricultural Policy (CAP), SPS will be replaced with a new scheme called the Basic Payment Scheme (BPS). It will start in 2005.

To apply for the Basic Payment Scheme (BPS), you'll need to:

  • be an 'active farmer'
  • have 5 hectares or more of eligible agricultural land
  • have 5 or more BPS entitlements
